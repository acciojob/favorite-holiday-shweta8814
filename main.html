Shimla

Shimla was built on top of seven hills: Inverarm Hill, Observatory Hill, Prospect Hill, Summer Hill, Bantony Hill, Elysium Hill and Jakhu Hill.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Fushimi Inari Shrine - Famous for its thousands of red torii gates that create a picturesque tunnel up the mountain.
  2. Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion) - A breathtaking temple covered in gold leaf, set against a tranquil pond.
  3. Kiyomizu-dera - Known for its wooden stage that offers panoramic views of the city and cherry blossoms.
  4. Arashiyama Bamboo Grove - An enchanting walk through towering bamboo stalks that creates a unique sensory experience.
  5. Gion District - Renowned for its traditional tea houses and the chance to spot a geisha in historic streets.
  • Kaiseki - A multi-course meal showcasing the best of seasonal ingredients and meticulous presentation.
  • Matcha - Japanese green tea that is both traditional and essential to the Kyoto experience.
  • Yudofu - A delicate tofu dish often enjoyed in the serene setting of a local temple.
  • Soba - Buckwheat noodles served either hot or cold, known for their simple yet satisfying flavor.
  • Takoyaki - Popular octopus-filled balls that are crispy on the outside and tender on the inside.
