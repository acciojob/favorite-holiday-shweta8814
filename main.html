Shimla

Shimla was built on top of seven hills: Inverarm Hill, Observatory Hill, Prospect Hill, Summer Hill, Bantony Hill, Elysium Hill, and Jakhu Hill. It is renowned for its picturesque landscapes and colonial architecture.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Summer Hill - Known for its scenic beauty and pleasant climate.
  2. Indian Institute of Advanced Studies - A stunning colonial building with historical significance.
  3. Annandale - A flat terrain perfect for outdoor activities and sports.
  4. Jakhoo Hill - Home to the Jakhoo Temple, offering panoramic views of Shimla.
  5. The Scandal Point, Ridge - A popular landmark with a vibrant atmosphere and great views.

Must-Try Foods

  • A Traditional Feast - A multi-course meal featuring local delicacies.
  • Siddu - A type of steamed bread often enjoyed with ghee or curry.
  • Chickpea Yogurt Curry - A rich and spicy dish that is a staple in local cuisine.
  • The Street Food Favorite - Includes a variety of snacks and sweets from local vendors.
  • A Tibetan Delight - Dishes such as momos and thukpa that reflect Shimla's Tibetan influence.
